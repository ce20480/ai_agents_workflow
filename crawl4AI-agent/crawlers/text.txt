##### Pretty, but difficult (5 jumbo)
It was hard for me to decide on how to rate this. I am an adult and I struggled to open all of mine. Some still only have a corner exposed. I had a hammer, chisel, and tried out several different tools in my house. Most of the tools I used are worn down and broken now FYI.I ended up having the most success hammering them as hard as possible on the concrete.I will say, I've read somewhere that the most difficult to open geodes tend to be the prettiest. My pictures don't do any justice, but mine are extremely beautiful and packed full of beautiful white quartz. One of them, I'm not sure of what it is, I see a little quartz on the edge mixed with pink and grey sparkly sediment filling the cross section. I am extremely pleased with the quality of what I have seen so far.I am rating 4 stars because I didn't expect it to be this difficult based on the description and other reviews. However, I do want to emphasize that the quality of the crystals inside have far exceeded my expectations. I also think any kid would have fun hammering these open with reckless abandon.Some specialized tools or other methods probably would have benefited me more with this activity.
